About This Interest

Why AI & Machine Learning?

My journey into artificial intelligence started with a simple question: can machines think? Here's what drives me.

💡

The Spark

I first encountered AI through a documentary on DeepMind's AlphaGo. Watching a machine master the ancient game of Go with creative moves that no human had ever played fascinated me. That moment ignited my passion for understanding how intelligence can emerge from code and data.

🎯

My Mission

I believe AI has the power to transform every sector — healthcare, agriculture, education, and beyond. My goal is to build intelligent systems that solve real-world problems, especially in underserved communities. From predicting crop yields to detecting diseases, I want to make AI accessible and impactful.
