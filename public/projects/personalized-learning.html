About This Interest

Why Personalized Learning?

Education is the most powerful tool for transforming lives. I believe AI can make personalized, high-quality education accessible to every student — regardless of their background, location, or learning pace.

🎓

Education for Everyone

In Bangladesh and many developing nations, students face overcrowded classrooms, limited teacher access, and one-size-fits-all curricula. AI-powered personalized learning systems can adapt to each student's unique needs — providing custom explanations, practice problems, and feedback that help them learn at their own pace. My dream is to make this technology available to every Bengali-speaking student.

🤖

AI as a Learning Companion

Imagine an AI tutor that knows exactly where you're struggling, adapts its teaching style to match your learning preferences, and provides instant feedback — available 24/7 at zero cost. Intelligent tutoring systems, powered by knowledge graphs and NLP, are making this vision a reality. I'm driven to build systems that augment teachers and empower learners.

Adaptive Learning Algorithms

Knowledge Graph Construction

Student Performance Prediction

📝

NLP for Education

📈

Learning Analytics & Dashboards

Visual analytics platforms that give teachers and students real-time insights into learning progress, engagement metrics, and areas needing improvement.
