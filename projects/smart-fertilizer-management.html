Problem Statement

The Fertilizer Problem

Overuse of fertilizers harms soil health and increases farming costs.

Implementation

How It Works

Turning soil data into precise fertilizer recommendations.

Step 01

📦 Soil Data Collection

Used open soil nutrient datasets from research repositories containing NPK levels, pH, and crop-specific requirements.

Step 02

🌲 Random Forest Model

Trained a Random Forest classifier to predict optimal fertilizer type and quantity based on soil and crop parameters.

Step 03

🌐 Flask Interface

Developed a simple Flask web interface that accepts soil inputs and delivers real-time fertilizer recommendations.

Step 04

📊 Sustainability Metrics

Displayed predictions alongside sustainability metrics including cost savings and environmental impact reduction.
