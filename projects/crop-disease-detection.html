Problem Statement

The Diagnosis Challenge

Manual crop disease diagnosis is time-consuming and often inaccurate.

Implementation

How It Works

From leaf images to disease identification in seconds.

Step 01

📸 Dataset Collection

Trained a CNN model using the PlantVillage dataset containing over 50,000 labeled leaf images across multiple disease categories.

Step 02

🖼️ Image Preprocessing

Preprocessed images with resizing, normalization, and augmentation techniques to ensure consistent accuracy across varied inputs.

Step 03

🌐 Flask Web App

Developed a Flask web application that accepts uploaded leaf images and runs the trained model for real-time disease predictions.

Step 04

📋 Results & Treatment

Displayed the predicted disease with confidence scores and suggested actionable treatment recommendations for farmers.

Disease Gallery

Common Leaf Diseases

The model can identify these common crop diseases with high accuracy.

🍂

Early Blight

Dark brown spots with concentric rings on lower leaves. Spreads rapidly in warm, wet conditions.

🍁

Late Blight

Water-soaked lesions that turn brown-black. Highly destructive, caused by Phytophthora infestans.

🌿

Leaf Mold

Pale green-yellow spots on upper surface with olive-green mold underneath. Common in high humidity.

🔴

Bacterial Spot

Small water-soaked spots turning dark brown with yellow halos. Spreads through rain and irrigation.
