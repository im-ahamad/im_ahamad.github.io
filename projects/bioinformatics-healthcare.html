About This Interest

Why Bioinformatics & Healthcare?

The intersection of biology, medicine, and artificial intelligence holds the key to solving humanity's greatest healthcare challenges.

🧬

The Beginning

My fascination with bioinformatics began when I realized how much genomic data remains unexplored. With the cost of DNA sequencing dropping exponentially, we now have unprecedented amounts of biological data. I wanted to build AI tools that can decode this information to diagnose diseases earlier, personalize treatments, and ultimately save lives.

🎯

My Vision

I envision a world where AI-powered healthcare is accessible to everyone, regardless of geography or economic status. From wearable health monitors that predict cardiac events to deep learning models that detect cancer from medical scans, I want to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and real-world clinical applications.
